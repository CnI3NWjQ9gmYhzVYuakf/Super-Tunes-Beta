About Us
Super Tunes is a discord music bot that provides music in a voice channel for any occasion regardless of you're dancing, gaming or vibing to some sweet tunes, Super Tunes is there to provide the audio you need. This all-new Discord Music Bot is made by music lovers, for music lovers. We designed this bot around the importance and responsibilities bots should have. Whether you're having a Gaming Date with your partner, or maybe a night with a few of your mates deeping a vibe at 3AM, Super Tunes is there to provide the audio you need.
