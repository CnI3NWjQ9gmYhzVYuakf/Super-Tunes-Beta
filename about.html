What is Super Tunes?

We are a bot that provides music in a voice channel for any occasion regardless if you are dancing, gaming or vibing to some sweet tunes.

You can use our service! Head here to invite us. 
Whether you're having a Gaming Date with your partner, or maybe a night with a few of your mates deeping a vibe at 3AM, Super Tunes is there to provide the audio you need.
This all new Discord Music Bot is made by music lovers, for music lovers. We designed this bot around the importancies and responsibilities bots should have. We've also made Super Tunes completely user friendly, providing a community server where you can meet other music veterans. You can join our very own discord server here!
If you feel pleased or satisfied with our music bots, then feel free to add one to your server by clicking here.
