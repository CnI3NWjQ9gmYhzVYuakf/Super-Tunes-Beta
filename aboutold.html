What is Super Tunes?

Who are we? We are a bot that provides music in a voice channel for any occasion regardless if you are dancing, gaming or vibing to some sweet tunes.

Don't need us to say anything else? Well, you can use our service! Head here to invite us. 

But, whether you just need to give yourself some headspace by listening to some tunes, or you just need to have  a vibe with your mates, Super Tunes is there, with its outstanding uptime and amazing music quality, competing with other top music bots.

This all new Discord Music Bot is made by music lovers, for music lovers. We designed this bot around the importance and responsibilities bots should have. We've also made Super Tunes completely user friendly, providing a community server where you can meet other music veterans, and constant updates on the bot in the server. You can join our very own discord server here!

If you feel pleased or satisfied with our music bots, then feel free to add one to your server by clicking here.

Also, finally, do you need any support? You can join our community for that too! Just click here for the 24/7 support experience!


We can't wait to see you using our bot or in our server.
